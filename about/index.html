About

Cornell 2025. Passionate about computing, data, and its applicability to a wide variety of fields. Looking to use technology to improve the lives of others.

Hello there! I’m Josh, and I grew up on Long Island, NY before coming to Cornell. In High School, I participated in predominantly math and science activities - my school’s research program (quite the time sink!), Science Olympiad, Mathletes, and more. However, when I did a summer research project using Python, I also became interested in coding, with an intense desire to learn more. I found it was a way to think both analytically and creatively, a combination not available in many fields. I could work on anything I wanted to, from game development to machine learning. Out of this came this very website, as I thought I should show off some of the CS and data science projects I have made. I love the combination of statistics and computers, and to that end I am double majoring in Computer Science and Operations Research (ORIE). I'm always looking for new opportunities in Software Engineering, Data Science, and Quantitative Trading, especially in roles where I can improve the lives of others. I’m also the president of Cornell Sports Analytics, and a senior editor in the Cornell Undergraduate Research Journal. In my free time, I am an avid runner and basketball player, and follow politics and elections very closely.
